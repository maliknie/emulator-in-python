Instruction Set:

NOP: 0000 0000 (No Operation)
LDA: 0001 0000 (Load to accumulator)
STA: 0010 0000 (Store accumulator value)
ADD: 0011 0000 (Add to accumulator)
SUB: 0100 0000 (substract from the accumulator)
JMP: 0101 0000 (Jump)
JZ : 0110 0000 (Jump if accumulator value is zero)
JNZ: 0111 0000 (Jump if accumulator value is not zero)
AND: 1000 0000 (Bitwise AND with accumulator)
OR : 1001 0000 (Bitwise OR with accumula tor)
HLT: 1010 0000 (Halt: Stop execution)
