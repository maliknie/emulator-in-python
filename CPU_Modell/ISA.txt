Instruction Set:

NOP: 0000 0000 (No Operation)
LDA: 0000 0001 (Load to accumulator)
STA: 0000 0010 (Store accumulator value)
ADD: 0000 0011 (Add to accumulator)
SUB: 0000 0100 (substract from the accumulator)
JMP: 0000 0101 (Jump)
JZ : 0000 0110 (Jump if accumulator value is zero)
JNZ: 0000 0111 (Jump if accumulator value is not zero)
AND: 0000 1000 (Bitwise AND with accumulator)
OR : 0000 1001 (Bitwise OR with accumulator)
HLT: 0000 1010 (Halt: Stop execution)
